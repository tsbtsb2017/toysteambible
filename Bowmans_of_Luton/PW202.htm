Notes: The Luton Bowmans were made by a company called Piece Parts and Assemblies from around 1946-50. The PW202 is arguably the hardest to find of their 3 stationary engines. This model was purchased at STiA 2008 and has be simpathetically refurbished to a high standard. It is shown here with its PW202 and PW203 brothers.
