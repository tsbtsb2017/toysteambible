Owner's website:
Notes: Eugene Beggs of the USA commenced making locomotives from about 1871, and made them well into the 20th century. Several types were made, with 4.2.0, 2.2.0, 4.2.2., and 4.4.0 wheel arrangements. These were made in considerable numbers.
