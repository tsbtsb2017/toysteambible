Notes: Mamod have made locomotives with oscillating cylinders in the past and this was their slide valve engine version. It is a surprisingly impressive runner though may not be too attractive for realistic garden rail modellers due to its 'toy look' . However, it makes a very affordable entry into the gas fired/slide-valve locomotive class and a good base for cosmetic modifications as can be seen in the last photo.
