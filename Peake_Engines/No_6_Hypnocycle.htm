Notes: This is the 6th engine in the ‘Peake’ family of models by Ben Peake of Australia and is based on the table engine design, it has a unique rotary valve arrangement driven via toothed gears connected the crankshaft of the flywheel, the engine is only single acting. Ben made a total of 51 engines and the first 5 of those came with a inline oiler as an added extra.
