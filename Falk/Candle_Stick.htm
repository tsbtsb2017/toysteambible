Falk Candle Stick

Images: _1_           TSB Entry:325
Approximate Date: pre-1914
Owner: John Chapman
Owner's website: http://www.steamtoys.co.uk
Notes: A 'candle stick' engine, made, in Nuremberg, by Josef Falk. It is marked 'Made in Germany' and so is probably pre WW1 (pre 1914). Engines imported into Britain after the war were not usually branded 'Made in Germany'.
