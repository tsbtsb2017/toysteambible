Notes: This is a new engine by Maxitrak, Maidstone Engineering. It is a gas fired 3/4 inch scale model of an Allchin Traction engine, built by William Allchin Globe works of Northampton about 1911. This is a powerfull engine capable of pulling scale loads, but will also tick over at slow speed.
