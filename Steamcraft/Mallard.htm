Steamcraft Mallard

Images: _1_ _2_ _3_ _4_           TSB Entry:648
Approximate Date: 1976
Owner: Benchmark
Owner's website: http://dampmaskiner.webs.com
Notes: This company should not be confused with 'Steam Craft Australia' . Steamcraft UK existed between 1976 and 1982 and was owned by David Taylor and quite a few different live steam models were made within this period It is claimed that only 180 examples of this particular live steam locomotive were made. The locomotive is OO/HO gauge and gas fired.
