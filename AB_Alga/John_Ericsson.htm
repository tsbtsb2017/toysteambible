Notes: This steam engine is complete with accessories. The accessories are very, very rare. When they show up, are usually sold very expensive. The original accessories consist of a grinder wheel with a polisher wheel combination, a transmission and a lathe. Quality is of the accessories is outstanding; just like the engine itself. John Ericsson steam engines were used in schools to teach youngsters. They have special safety features not found in any other steam engine.
