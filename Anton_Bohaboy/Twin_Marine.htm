Notes: This addition pretty much completes all the engines Anton Bohaboy produced over his 45 years of being in business.
This is his largest Twin Marine with 1" Bore and 1" Stroke. Engine is 7 1/2" x 6 1/2 x 3 1/2 and weighs about 7lbs. This engine was produced bet. 1940-1955 and was primarily used to power a 6'-8' launch.
The incredible craftsmanship Bohaboy possessed allows this engine to run at .20Psi at about 40 RPM's. Very robust and clearly an engine built to last.
