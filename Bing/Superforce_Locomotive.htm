Notes: Small Bing `superforce` locomotive made about 1926. The single cylinder and flywheel can be seen in the cab. A smooth and steady runner. A quaint thing is that one side of the cab is letterred `London Midland Scottish` but the other side has `London Midland Schottish`. The Germans made a minor error!
