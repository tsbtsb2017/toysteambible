Notes: Here we have an early 1950's Jensen meths fired 75 steam engine. This engine is the only meths fired Jensen to be made. Distinguishable by the air vent holes in the firebox sides and no stack fitted to the rear. Engine unit was later used on the Jensen 70 models, has an electric model plate fitted but is stamped with the model designation 75. Meths burner is made of tin with a cork stopper, it is rare to find a Jensen 75 still with its burner.
