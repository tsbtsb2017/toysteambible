Notes: Very early Jensen 60, likely one of the first. Features that make it different from later 60's include: square cornered base (it's believed Jensen bought bases from Wilesco for these first 60's), overflow screw (instead of sight glass window), copper-plated steel firebox (not painted), and lack of a steam regulator.
