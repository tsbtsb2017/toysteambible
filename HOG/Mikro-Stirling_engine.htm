Notes: Lovely detail and a stunning runner. HOG is one of the primary makers of modern Sterling engines and they utilize a Beam engine design that runs at incredible rpm...so fast it is but a blur while it runs almost silently off a flame the size of a match. Thes can be found on eBay and popular online steam engine retailers...though there is no steam and no water used...just a flame.
