Stuart Turner OH1

Images: _1_ _2_ _3_           TSB Entry:885
Approximate Date: 2013
Owner: Swift Fox
Owner's website: http://swiftfoxsteamco.webs.com
Notes: This engine was produced by Stuart for a short time in the 1930’s and it was part of a range of six engines under the ‘Progress’ name. In 2013 Stuart reintroduced the OH1 as a set of castings to machine, the only difference between the new model being that the cylinder/covers and engine standard are made of cast iron instead of the gunmetal of the original.
