Notes: This is my early 60's ST Hammer mounted on 1/4" Aluminum plate and a wood plinth. I had to cut, route and stain the base to have a solid and presentable base for this powerful Hammer. Note the Manual pump oiler Reservoir and drain cock on opposite side of piece.
This piece stands 11.5" tall by 3.75" wide by 7.5" long and weighs about 10 Lbs without base. It operated optimally on 30-40psi and can strike a powerful blow to a gentle tap with control of the operating handle.
