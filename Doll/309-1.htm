Approximate Date: c1935
Owner: Silver Fox Steam
Owner's website:
Notes: A small overtype engine with 'locomotive' style chimney fired by a single wick spirit burner. The burner sits in a cut-out in the base, centrally under the boiler. This is the type of solid, compact unit that may have inspired the later Mamod MM series engines. Lovely pressed 'brick-effect' firebox.
