Doll 310-5

Images: _1_ _2_ _3_ _4_           TSB Entry:486
Approximate Date: c1930
Owner: Silver Fox Steam
Owner's website:
Notes: A vertical engine, generally termed a 'candlestick'! This is an example of the largest of the five '310' series engines. It sports a 'blued' brass boiler and nickel-plated whistle & cylinder. (There was also a 305 series which were indentical except for the finish on boilers and cylinders - they were polished and laquered.) The engine came with instructions, pourer, filler funnel and burner.
