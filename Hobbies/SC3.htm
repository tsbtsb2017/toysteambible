Notes: Produced in and around 1939/40 (Mamod and Hobbies variants) these SC engines featured lineshafts screwed on to their bases. With the deletion of the countershaft on the SC3/3/4 they are easy to identify but very very rare. No exact numbers are know to have been built. Owned by Mamodman
