Approximate Date: 2009
Owner: Benchmark
Owner's website: http://dampmaskiner.webs.com/
Notes: Märklin offered this engine on a 'subscription only' basis, for a period of time (which was extended at least once) between 2004-2005. After the deadline date, no more orders were accepted and production ceased. This is why Märklin claimed it was a 'limited edition'. They were reproductions of the Marklin 4158 Compound that was made from 1911 to 1939.
