Marklin 4149-9

Approximate Date: 1919
Owner: Dave's Engine Room
Owner's website:
Notes: My favorite Marklin. This large flywheeled single cylinder is, in a way, the same in comparison of the 4158 like the Wilesco D28 and D32. With the 9cm dia. boiler and a valve to control the steam outlet, this machine just roars when placed under full steam. It is a stout but very well-balanced machine. It's rarity is a bit of pity, it's a wonderful engine.
