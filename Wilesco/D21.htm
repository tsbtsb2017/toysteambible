Notes: This is a large engine the base measures 300mm x 350mm. This model has a water injection hand pump, so in theory you can run the engine indefinitely. Water is pumped from a reservoir underneath. The exhaust steam is fed to the chimney and the condensed water collected at the base. It is equipped with a working steam pressure gauge, drain valve and steam regulator valve. The engine runs nicely at low revs.
