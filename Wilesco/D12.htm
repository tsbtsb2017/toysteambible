Notes: Here is my refurbished Wilesco D12 ca. 1960. She was given to me by a kind member of MamodForums.co.uk who offered it to anyone new to the steam hobby. When I got her, she needed quite a bit of love. The filler plug and stack are replacements, and the nickle plating on the boiler was so bad, I chose to take it all the way back to brass. The last picture is prior to restoration. The refurbishing thread is here.
