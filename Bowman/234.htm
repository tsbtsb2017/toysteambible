2 Bowman 234

Images: _1_           TSB Entry:168
Approximate Date: circa 1930 - 1935
Owner: Roly Williams
Owner's website: http://rolywilliams.com/bowman_234.htm
Notes: This is the top of the range of Bowmans locos. The tender carries the number LNER 4472, which was the number carried by LNER's Flying Scotsman. The number is about the only similarity with the famous loco. When I bought this engine I was told it was from c1948, but I have since been told that the plain con rods date it much earlier.
